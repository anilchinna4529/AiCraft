AICraft was born in Hyderabad with one mission — make powerful AI automation accessible to every business, in every sector.

Our Story

AICraft was founded in 2023 at Kondapur, Hyderabad — one of India's fastest-growing tech hubs. We saw a clear gap: powerful AI tools existed, but they were either too expensive, too complex, or too generic for most businesses.

We set out to build a marketplace of purpose-built AI automation tools — each designed for a specific industry, with real business outcomes in mind. Not AI for AI's sake, but AI that actually gets work done.

Today, AICraft serves businesses across Healthcare, Finance, Education, Manufacturing, and 8 other sectors — helping teams automate workflows, reduce costs, and move faster than ever before.
