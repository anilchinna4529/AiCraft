Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Introduction

AICraft ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, and interact with our API.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you sign up for an AICraft account, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Company/Organization name
  • Industry sector
  • Password (encrypted)

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about your interactions with AICraft:

  • API calls and requests
  • Tokens used and consumption patterns
  • Tools accessed and features used
  • Login times and device information
  • IP addresses and user agent data

Content You Provide

When you use our AI tools, we process the text, data, and prompts you submit to generate outputs. This content is used to provide the service and improve model accuracy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process API requests and authenticate users
  • Monitor and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, password resets, announcements)
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize platform performance
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries

4. Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All communication is protected with TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data is encrypted with AES-256
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control and least-privilege policies
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Incident Response: 24/7 monitoring and rapid response protocols

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. Account data can be deleted upon request. Login logs are retained for 90 days. API usage data is retained for 12 months for billing and analytics purposes.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Stripe), email service (Resend), hosting (Render)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In the event of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

7. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@aicraft.co.in.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to maintain user sessions and store authentication tokens in browser localStorage. We do not use third-party tracking or analytics cookies for marketing purposes.

9. Data Processing Agreement

Enterprise customers can request a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) outlining how we process data on their behalf. Contact legal@aicraft.co.in for details.
